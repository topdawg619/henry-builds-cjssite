Process

Concierge-level detail in 8 deliberate moves.

Borrowed from AMMO-style checklists and CJ's own SOPs—each minute is mapped so owners see exactly what happens in their driveway.

  1. 0-5 min

    Site prep & shade check

    Park vehicle out of direct sun, run power/water, tape delicate trim, capture intake photos.

  2. 5-20 min

    Citrus pre-rinse + wheel detox

    pH-balanced pre-soak, iron remover on barrels, detail brush around lugs, rinse top-down.

  3. 20-35 min

    Two-bucket wash & contact decon

    Microfiber mitt, grit guards, and lubricated shampoo keep soft paint safe before clay and BOOST topper.

  4. 35-45 min

    Drying pass

    Forced-air blowers chase water out of trim; dedicated towels finish with lubrication so no towel marring.

  5. 45-60 min

    Interior reset

    Vacuum map, steam plastics, enzyme spot treating, leather nourishment, ozone on request.

  6. 60-75 min

    Protection stack

    Spray sealant or ceramic topper, glass polish, tire dressing tuned for your finish (satin, matte, gloss).

  7. 75-85 min

    Inspection & documentation

    Before/after photos, maintenance notes, filter log for memberships.

  8. 85+ min

    Client walkthrough

    Texted media reel, in-person tour, and post-care plan so the gloss lasts.

Shop rules

CJ's detailing commandments

  • Never dry bare. Microfiber + lubrication or nothing.
  • Ceramic-safe chemistry only. Every shampoo and degreaser is checked against your coating before it's opened.
  • Hands-off interiors. Gloves, fresh towels, and ozone filtration keep cabins hypoallergenic.
  • Documentation or it didn’t happen. Membership logs capture every product used, so you always know what’s on the paint.

Questions

Everything clients ask before booking.

Do you need my power or water?

No. Vans carry filtered water, power, lighting, and reclaim mats. Let us know if your HOA has special requirements and we’ll send documentation.

What about rain or marine layer?

We monitor Doppler + coastal humidity. If weather won’t let coatings cure, we reschedule within 24 hours at no charge.

Can you work in garages or lots?

Yes—office garages, school lots, booster club fundraisers, and dealership overflow are common. We’ll bring mats + cones for tidy setups.

Do you offer fleet or athletic pricing?

Fleet, coaching staffs, and booster organizations get tiered pricing + consolidated billing. Mention it in the form and we’ll set it up.
